## 1. /ɪ/

#### 1.1 Words have this sound

|  Word  |  Sound    |
|--------|-----------|
|  kit   |  /kɪt/    |
|  bid   |  /bɪd/    |
|  hymn  |  /hɪm/    |
| minute |  /ˈmɪnɪt/ |

#### 1.2 Similar sounds

|    /ɪ/    |     /iː/     |
|-----------|--------------|
| hit /hɪt/ | heat  /hiːt/ |
| lip /lɪp/ | leap  /liːp/ |
| sick /sɪk/| seek  /siːk/ |
| will /wɪl/| wheel /wiːl/ |

## 5. /ə/

#### 5.1 Words have this sound

|    Word     |    Sound      |
|-------------|---------------|
|    a        |    /ə/        |
|    the      |    /ðə/       |
|    of       |    /əv/       |
|    that     |    /ðət/      |
|    about    |    /əˈbaʊt/   |
|    common   |    /ˈcɒmən/   |
|    standard |    /ˈsdændəd/ |
|    butter   |    /ˈbʌtə/    |

#### 5.2 schwa /ʃwɑː/

* e.g. unstress syllables

|   Word  |   Sound   |
|---------|-----------|
|  accuse | /əˈkjuːz/ |
|  banana | /bəˈnɑːnə |
|  father | /ˈfɑːðə/  |
|  camera | /ˈkæmrə/  |

* e.g. schwa in phrase

|     Phrase     |       Sound       |
|----------------|-------------------|
| a cup of tea   |   /ə ˈkʌpə tiː/   |
| a piece of cake |   /ə ˈpiːsə keik/ |
